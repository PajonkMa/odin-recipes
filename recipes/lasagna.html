Lasgana Recipe

Description

A classic lasagna recipe with layers of pasta, sauce, cheese, and meat.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Cook the meat
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the cheese sauce
  4. Cook the lasagna
